About Me — A bold, generous introduction

I am Ashrakt Ahmed, a passionate student of Early Childhood Education with a relentless curiosity for design, storytelling and building delightful digital experiences. My journey started in classrooms and children's workshops, where I learned that learning must be playful, intentional, and beautifully crafted. Over the years I combined that pedagogical mindset with web design — bringing educational ideas to life through clear layouts, subtle motion and friendly micro-interactions.

I love turning complex concepts into approachable, interactive pages: lesson prototypes, children's learning games, and polished portfolios. I enjoy working across the entire creation process — from rapid ideation and hand sketches, to pixel-perfect HTML/CSS and lightweight JavaScript. I aim for work that is warm, trustworthy and slightly unexpected — a balance between rigor and wonder.

Outside of development and design, I spend time reading child development research, practicing visual storytelling, and experimenting with sound effects that make small moments in a website feel alive. I value collaboration, iterative feedback, and a strong attention to detail. If you're looking for someone who brings compassion, craft and play to every project — that's me.

Quick facts: Skilled at responsive layout, accessibility-first components, CSS animation, basic JavaScript logic, and creating educational interfaces for young learners. Fast learner, reliable communicator, and always excited to try new visual approaches.
